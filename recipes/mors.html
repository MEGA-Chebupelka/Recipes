Mors

Description

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make Mors:

  1. Rinse the cranberries and drain.
  2. Smash with a stomper.
  3. Place the smashed cranberries in cheesecloth and squeeze the juice out into a non-oxidizing container.
  4. Take the cranberry cake out of the cheesecloth and place into a big pot, then add the sugar and the water and stir.
  5. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes, then remove from the heat and let cool down.
  6. Pour through a couple of layers of cheesecloth into a gallon container fitted with a funnel. Add the juice.
  7. Place in a fridge and let chill completely before serving.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you’ll need to make this mors recipe:

  • 2 lbs cranberries
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 gallon water
